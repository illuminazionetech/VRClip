<b>Features</b>
Download videos and audio from the platforms supported by yt-dlp
Watch downloads in the built-in player with automatic flat/360/180/3D detection
Fully immersive playback on Meta Quest through the Meta Spatial SDK
OpenGL sphere rendering with touch and gyroscope panning on phones and tablets
Embed metadata, subtitles, and thumbnails into downloaded files
Download whole playlists with one click
Use the embedded aria2c as external downloader
Execute custom yt-dlp commands with templates
Material 3 Expressive UI with dynamic color theming
No ads, no tracking, no accounts
